Statehouse reporting

I currently cover the Missouri legislature as an advanced statehouse reporter at The Columbia Missourian. This is the second session in a row on the state government beat, and it's the best reporting job I've ever had. Covering the statehouse redefined what journalism meant to me, and it's my dream job to cover a state legislature full-time.
