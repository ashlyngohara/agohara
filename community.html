Community reporting

I have over three years of hyperlocal community reporting experience. From 2015 to 2017, I was a reporting intern at Our Hometown Magazine in my hometown of Chula Vista, California (south San Diego County). My work ranged from writing profiles of community members, to crafting feature stories, to writing community news stories.

At The Columbia Missourian, I worked as a public life reporter (while also covering the 2018 midterm election) in the city of Columbia, Missouri. As a public life reporter, I covered city council meetings, wrote feature stories about local events and wrote about different city and county elections.
