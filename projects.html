Special projects

As the field of journalism becomes increasingly diigital, it is important to me to experiment with different digital storytelling techniques in my work. This includes digital photo essays, coding story websites and even staging in-person exhibits where people can interact with news in person.





OFF-SEASON

A transmedia journalism project that looks at Coney Island during it's off-season. Website coded and images taken by me as part of a larger transmedia project called: Identity NYC



Playground

A website that I built to experiment with different ways of presenting information online.



Visualizing Data Midterm

Midterm data visualization project that attempts to answer the question: is the news media really biased against Donald Trump? Visualizations created in Adobe InDesign.



ELEVATORU

Transmedia journalism project about elevators at The New School. Full project includes interviews, audio segments, a photo essay, an exhibit and a website. Website designed by me.



Yeah, it's possible to learn a new language using Duolingo, but probably not by just using the app

Final data visualization project that answers whether or not it is possible to "learn" a language using the app Duolingo, as evidence by their user data. Visualizations created using Adobe Illustrator.
