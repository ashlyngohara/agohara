Graphics

Data visualization is one of my favorite parts of being a data journalist. Part math, part journalism, and part art, data viz can be extremeley powerful in helping show trends, communicating complex ideas or complementing a number-heavy story. I strongly believe in the ability of sophisticated visual elements to make news stories more dynamic and accessible, and always try to pitch visual components with stories that would otherwise be text-only.
