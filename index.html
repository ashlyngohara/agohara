Hello! Welcome to my website. My name is Ashlyn O'Hara and I am a senior at the University of Missouri School of Journalism studying data journalism. I'm a statehouse and graphics reporter atThe Columbia Missourian and a graphics reporter at Missouri Business Alert.


From Fall 2017 to Spring 2018, I studied Journalism + Design and Politics at the Eugene Lang College of Liberal Arts at The New School in New York City, where I also wrote for me college newspaper, The New School Free Press.


My journalism career started atOur Hometown Magazine in San Diego, where I worked as an intern for two years, and at the LA Times' HS Insider program where i was a contributing author during Summer 2016.
